JOLLOF RICE

INGREDIENTS

  • groundnut oil
  • pepper
  • onions
  • onga spice
  • ginger and garli
  • salt
  • maggie
  • tomatoes
  • meat water

STEPS

  1. parboil the rice
  2. then fry your tomatoes mixed with the ingredients except the meat water
  3. after 7 mins of frying add the meat water
  4. and boil for 10 mins on low heat
  5. after 10 mins add your paroiled rice
  6. and boil till water dry off
  7. add your fried meat if any
  8. lastly enjoy your meal
